Vol. I — On why this exists.
Dear Reader,
We started Asterix because we were tired.
We missed the old way. Where a connection took effort to build and regret to sever. The way people once fell in love with minds before bodies. With margins before faces. With the quiet weight of someone’s sentences before the sound of their voice.
So we built something different.
Asterix is not another dating app. It is a literary correspondence deliberate, slow, and intimate by design. Here, you will meet the personality before meeting the person. Not because we want to limit you, but because we refuse to dilute what is rare.
You will first encounter their thoughts. The books that broke them. The lines they defend with their whole heart. The way they see the world when no one is watching. Only later does the face gently clarify like ink slowly becoming visible on aged paper.
We do not optimize for volume. We optimize for resonance.
This place is for those who still underline passages. Who copy sentences into private notebooks. Who believe that real intimacy cannot be rushed, only carefully drafted. For those who understand that some of the most beautiful stories begin not with a photograph, but with a single sentence that refuses to leave you.
If you’re here, we already know something about you.
You are the kind of person who would rather read a paragraph than send a sticker. Who still believes mystery is the beginning of romance. Who is willing to wait for something worth waiting for.
Thank you for claiming your card.
Your experience arrives soon.
Until then, keep reading slowly.
With quiet reverence,
The Editors of Asterix
